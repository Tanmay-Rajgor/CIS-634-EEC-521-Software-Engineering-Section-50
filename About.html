Our Cause

Education is a key to change one’s life. Education improves one’s knowledge, skills and develops the personality and attitude. Education is a never ending process. It transforms us completely from inside and outside by changing our mind and personality as well as improving our confidence level. It changes our life completely as it is constructive in nature.  Education helps us to determine about good and bad things. An educated person having good education becomes a good citizen in the society.

In many developing countries, families often cannot afford to send their children to school, leaving millions of children of school-age deprived of education. There are often indirect costs associated with education, such as for school books, uniforms or travel, that prevent children from low-income families accessing school.  In order to overcome this, international human rights law said primary education is compulsory and free of charge. Secondary and higher education shall be made progressively free of charge.

The most important benefit of “free education” is accessibility. Since there are no financial burdens, everyone can study no matter if you’re rich or poor.

Free education is necessary in India, as most poverty stricken families do not have the money and the means to send their kids to school. In India, many kids after the age of 7 are seen working. Families in urgent need of money find it more productive to send their kids to be employed rather than sending them to school. Free education will therefore give these families the opportunity to send their kids to school. While under the labor laws of India, employing a child before the age of fourteen is illegal, however, child labor is still in practice as the laws aren't strict. Education gives these kids the opportunity to strive for social mobility.

Though children are getting free education, the quality of education, the facility required for the education system is still lacking behind. Our motive is to provide required environments for children's education and to improve current existing educational quality.

What we do?

We currently focus on school development, education development and children's development. Under school development, we try to bring out the necessary requirements for each individual to study. Under Educational development, we focus on the quality of education that each individual student should get. Under Children development, we focus on the necessary basic, social and daily life etiquette's.

So we have taken an initiative to organize the above mentioned improvements by adopting schools whose conditional requirements are lacking behind.

The future scope of this organization is to maintain a constant and consistent improvements in the development of school, children and education. For the development of children, we mentor each individual student to achieve their goals in their life.
