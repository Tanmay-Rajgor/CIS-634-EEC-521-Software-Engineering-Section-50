Our Programs

Swachh Vidayalaya

Swachh Vidyalaya is aimed to ensure every school has a set of functioning and well maintained water, sanitation and hygiene facilities. This program helps to produce a healthy school environment and to develop or support appropriate health and hygiene behaviors.

A clean and healthy school creates a cycle of opportunities, improved hygiene, improved health & less disease, improved attendance & decreased drop-out rate, better student performance and economic growth.

SchoolsForSDGs

The SchoolsForSDGs campaign is a global movement to aid the realization of the immense potential power that schools hold in catalyzing the sustainable development agenda for 2030, by fully leveraging the communities and resources that schools converge.

This initiative is aimed at promoting awareness and understanding of the Global Goals, and at encouraging schools to take measurable action towards achieving them, by empowering and enabling schools through knowledge resources, incentive programs, community engagements.
